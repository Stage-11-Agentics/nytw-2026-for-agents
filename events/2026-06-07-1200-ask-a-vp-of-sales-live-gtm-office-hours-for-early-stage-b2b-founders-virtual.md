## Description

​Building your sales and marketing systems can feel overwhelming. These office hours are an opportunity to ask questions, troubleshoot challenges, and get practical advice on how to grow your startup.

​Join Kat Rucker, Fractional VP of Sales and Marketing, for small-group office hours focused on early-stage go-to-market strategy.

​Common topics include:

​• Founder-led sales
• Building early pipeline and outreach
• Messaging and positioning
• Turning conversations into revenue
• Prioritizing sales and marketing when you have limited time and resources

​Bring your current questions or challenges, and we’ll work through them together.

​How the Sessions Work
​These are small group office hours designed to keep the discussion practical and interactive.

Host will message you with a recommended 15- minute time slot during the hour, but feel free to come and go as you please.

​Each time block functions as drop-in group office hours, so you’re welcome to join for the full session or come with a specific question.

​About Kat
​Kat Rucker is the founder of Kat Rucker Consulting Group and a Fractional VP of Sales who works with early-stage startups on go-to-market strategy, sales systems, and founder-led growth.

​She has over 10 years of experience in B2B sales across software and services and helps startups build their first scalable revenue processes.
